Delete your account

You can delete your Ivy Spaces account directly from the app. All your data will be removed immediately and permanently.

⚠️
This action is permanent. All private notes are removed from your device. Shared workspaces you own will be deactivated and all workspace data — notes, members, trash — will be deleted.

Delete from the app

Recommended
  1. Open Ivy Spaces on your device and sign in.
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner of the home screen.
  3. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete account.
  4. Re-enter your password to confirm your identity.
  5. Tap Delete permanently. Your account and all data are removed immediately.
or

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or your account, send a deletion request from your registered email address and we will delete your account within 30 days.

Send deletion request

Send from your registered email address so we can verify ownership. We'll confirm once your account has been removed.
