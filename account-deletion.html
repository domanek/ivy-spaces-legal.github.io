Delete your account

You can delete your Ivy Spaces account directly from the app. This page explains how, and provides an email fallback if you can't access the app.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. All your private notes will be removed from your device. Shared workspaces you own will be deactivated and all workspace data will be deleted.

Delete from the app Recommended

  1. Open Ivy Spaces on your device and sign in.
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner.
  3. Scroll down to Account and tap Delete account.
  4. Re-enter your password to confirm your identity.
  5. Tap Delete permanently. Your account and all associated data will be removed immediately.
or

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or your account, send us an email from the address registered with your Ivy Spaces account and we will delete it within 30 days.

✉ Send deletion request

Please send the request from your registered email address so we can verify ownership. We will confirm once your account has been removed.
