2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is processed and stored via Google Firebase:

  • Location: Google Cloud – Europe (Frankfurt, Germany)
  • Firebase Authentication — login
  • Cloud Firestore — notes and user profiles
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting

Subscription and purchase management is handled by RevenueCat and Google Play. Their respective privacy policies apply: RevenueCat Privacy Policy · Google Privacy Policy

3. Legal Basis

Data is processed based on your consent through use of the app (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and for the performance of our service agreement (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is only shared with the infrastructure providers listed above (Google, RevenueCat) solely for the purpose of operating the app.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data upon request
  • Object to data processing

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from the app. All your data — private notes, workspace memberships, and profile — is removed immediately and permanently.

  1. Open Ivy Spaces on your device and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Tap Delete permanently. Your account and all data are removed immediately.
or

If you no longer have access to the app or your account, send a deletion request from your registered email address and we will delete your account within 30 days.

✉ Send deletion request

Send from your registered email address so we can verify ownership. We'll confirm once your account has been removed.
